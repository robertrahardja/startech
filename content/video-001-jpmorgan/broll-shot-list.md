## Notes on selection

- Pick **1080p** versions, not 4K (faster to work with, indistinguishable on YouTube)
- Avoid anything with hard cuts to faces — you want neutral background visuals
- Avoid clips with on-screen text in another language
- Avoid anything dated (no 2010s phone models, no old computer monitors)

## Filename convention

Keep the `broll-NN-name.mp4` pattern. CapCut will sort them automatically and you'll
know which goes where without referring back to this doc.
