# Thumbnail Brief — Video 001

## Goal

Stop the scroll on YouTube's home feed. The thumbnail must read in <0.5 seconds.

## Dimensions

1280 × 720 (Canva preset: "YouTube Thumbnail")

## Layout

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                 │
│   $19.8B           [silhouette of               │
│   JPMORGAN          businessman                 │
│   AI BET            looking at                  │
│                     rising chart]               │
│                                                 │
│   ────────                                      │
│                                                 │
│   What SMBs                                     │
│   Should Copy →                                 │
│                                                 │
│                              [StarTech logo]    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Exact text

**Top left, huge bold**:
> $19.8B
> JPMORGAN
> AI BET

(Each on its own line. "$19.8B" in red. "JPMORGAN" and "AI BET" in white.)

**Bottom left, smaller**:
> What SMBs
> Should Copy →

(In yellow/gold to match StarTech brand. The arrow is part of the text.)

**Bottom right corner, small**:
> StarTech logo (use your existing logo PNG from `public/assets/startech-logo-full.png`)

## Right half — image

Either:
- Option A: silhouette of a businessman/woman looking at a rising line chart (Canva → search "businessman silhouette chart")
- Option B: stylised stack of cash with a small office building beside it (Canva → search "money business")

Use whichever looks cleaner. Don't pick anything cartoonish or stock-photo cliché.

## Background

Dark navy gradient (top-left lighter, bottom-right darker). NOT pure black — that makes
white text vibrate. Use `#0F172A` to `#020617` as a gradient.

## Fonts in Canva

- Headline ("$19.8B JPMORGAN AI BET"): **Anton** or **Bebas Neue** (free in Canva, very bold)
- Subhead ("What SMBs Should Copy"): **Inter Bold** or **Montserrat Bold**

## Brand colors to use

- Gold/yellow accent: `#E5B53C` (matches your st-gold-light)
- Red for the dollar amount: `#DC2626`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Background dark: `#0F172A` to `#020617`

## What to avoid

- No emoji on the thumbnail (cheap)
- No "MUST WATCH" / "SHOCKING" — clickbait words are 2026 demonetization signals
- No arrow drawn ON the silhouette pointing at the chart (cliché)
- No more than 6 words of text total — anything more won't read at mobile size

## Export

PNG, max quality. File name: `thumbnail.png` in this folder.

## Mobile test

Before uploading, view it at 320px wide (Canva has a preview). If the text is unreadable,
make it bigger and remove words.
